Our Mission

Resol Global Health is a non-profit organization (501(c)3 status pending) with a three-fold mission to decrease health disparities and promote health and wellness in low-resource communities.

  • Reduce underlying causes of illness
  • Increase capacity of healthcare workers
  • Improve access to healthcare

Featured Projects

  • Community-based health education for women
  • Water filtration access with Ecofiltros
  • Mobile medical clinics (jornadas)
  • Midwife training programs
