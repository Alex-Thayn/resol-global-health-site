Chicacao, Guatemala

Our current focus is the Chicacao region in southern Guatemala. We work alongside Spanish and Mayan language speakers, centering local leadership and knowledge.

  • Travel to clinics ranges from an hour by foot to several hours by road.
  • Schooling averages 3 to 4 years, with many women attending 1 to 2 years.
  • Many communities lack sewage systems, garbage disposal, and clean water.
  • Many workers earn about 2,000 quetzales per month, about 260 USD.
